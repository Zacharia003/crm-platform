## Objective

Build a simple, reliable CRM for small businesses to track enquiries,
orders, invoices, and payments from one system.

## Included Modules

- User Authentication & Roles (Admin, User)
- Mail View & Reply (manual sync)
- Lead / Enquiry Management (stage-based)
- Orders Management
- Purchase Orders
- Proforma Invoice & Sales Invoice (PDF)
- Manual Payment Status
- File Attachments (email, order, invoice)

## Excluded (Phase 2+)

- Email automation
- Mobile applications
- GST / E-way bill integration
- Advanced analytics
- Notifications & alerts
